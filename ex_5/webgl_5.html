Play with Planets!

Gravity is awesome.

Use your left mouse button to turn the camera. The right mouse button to strafe and the mousewheel to zoom in and out.

Reset Planets

Press this button to reset Planets. Planets will be created with random sizes atrandom positions

Reset Planets on XY Plane

Pressing this will also reset the planets. Their initial positions however will be randomly distributed on the XY plane only.

Number of initial Planets

This is the number of planets that will be generated when you press one of the reset buttons

Show FPS

When checked some debug output will be shown. Also a Framerate counter will be displayed in the upper left corner.

Change G

Change the value of the gravitational constant here.Bigger means faster movement and stronger forces.
